My Sticky Rice and spam recipe.

This is a simple and easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Spam
  • Soy sauce
  • terriyaki sauce
  • Green onions
  • Spinicha
  • kimchi

How to make the Sticky Rice.

  • Add the sticky rice to a mixing bowl or other deep container.
    Fill it with water so that the water level is at least 3 inches above the rice,
    as the rice will expand when soaking; Soak for 6 to 24 hours.
    Soak it longer if you want your sticky rice to have a softer texture.
