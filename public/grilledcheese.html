Grilled cheese time!

I hope your grilled cheese comes out awesome!

Ingredients for grilled cheese

  • italian bread.
  • a slice of sharp chaddar cheese.
  • a slice of colby jack.
  • butter.
  • Spinach.
  • bacon
  • sundried tomatoes

How to make the griled cheese.

  • Start by cooking the bacon; make sure the bacon is cooked on medium heat so you don't burn it.
    make sure you cook both side 4-5 minuites on each side.
  • After cooking the bacon make sure you warm up your sundried tomatoes; you can also cook them
    on medium heat as well, you can also add your Spinach to the pan to cook as well.
  • Make sure to butter your bread on one side each, put one slice on the pan on medium heat,
    then add your bacon, sundried tomatoes, and Spinach; Then add another your other side of the bread.

There you have a finished grilled cheese! hope you enjoy!
